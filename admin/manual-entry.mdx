Manual entry allows administrators and clinicians to add screening records that were collected on paper rather than through the online assessment flow. Each record is validated against per-factor maximums before being inserted into Supabase.

<Note>
  Records created through manual entry are tagged with a `MANUAL` source badge in the data table so they can be distinguished from online submissions.
</Note>

## When to use manual entry

Use manual entry when:

- A child completed the assessment on a printed questionnaire rather than the digital form.
- An assessment was conducted in a low-connectivity setting and the scores were recorded on paper.
- You are migrating historical paper records into the platform.

## Step-by-step guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the manual entry modal">
    In the admin dashboard header, click the **Añadir Registro Manual** button. The modal opens with an empty form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter demographic information">
    Fill in the child's demographic fields:

    - **Edad** — age in years (number)
    - **Género** — select Niño or Niña
    - **Año de nacimiento** — four-digit birth year
    - **Diagnósticos** — any relevant clinical diagnoses (free text)
    - **Tipo de test** — select Escolar or Preescolar
    - **Fecha** — date the assessment was administered
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the test type">
    The factor fields shown in the next section of the form change depending on whether you selected **Preescolar** or **Escolar**. Ensure you have selected the correct type before entering scores.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter factor scores">
    For each phase, enter the raw score obtained for every factor. The form displays the maximum allowed value next to each field. Scores cannot exceed their factor maximum — the form will reject the entry if any value is out of range.

    See the factor tables below for all factor names and their maximums.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the record">
    Click **Guardar**. The scores are summed into phase totals and the record is inserted into the `answers` table in Supabase. The data table refreshes automatically to show the new entry.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Validation rules

The manual entry form enforces the following rules before saving:

- Every factor score must be a non-negative integer.
- No factor score may exceed its defined maximum (see tables above).
- Age and birth year must be valid numbers.
- Test type and gender are required selections.

<Warning>
  If any factor score exceeds its maximum, the form will display a validation error and the record will not be saved. Check the entered values against the factor tables above.
</Warning>

## What happens after saving

1. Factor scores within each phase are summed into a phase total (F1, F2, F3, F4).
2.     The record is inserted into the `answers` table with a `source: 'manual'` marker.
3. The associated child profile is created or linked in the `users` table.
4. The data table in the dashboard refreshes to include the new record, which will display the `MANUAL` badge in the source column.
