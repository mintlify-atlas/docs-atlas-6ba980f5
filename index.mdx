## Bienvenido a la plataforma AVDB

**Cribado de Desarrollo Infantil** is a web-based pediatric developmental screening platform that administers standardized assessments of daily living activities (Actividades de la Vida Diaria Básicas — AVDB) for children aged 3–12.

The platform supports two assessment tracks and evaluates children across four key developmental dimensions, providing clinicians and researchers with anonymous, structured data and an analytics dashboard.

## Four assessment dimensions

Every assessment — regardless of age group — evaluates the child across four developmental dimensions:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Alimentación" icon="utensils" href="/guides/scoring">
    Oral sensitivity, manual dexterity during meals, and social eating behaviors.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Higiene Personal" icon="hand-sparkles" href="/guides/scoring">
    Bladder/bowel control, tooth brushing, personal care, and grooming.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Vestido" icon="shirt" href="/guides/scoring">
    Dressing tasks, clothing management, and sensory tolerance for garments.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Funcionamiento Diario" icon="brain" href="/guides/scoring">
    Basic and higher-order executive functions, routine management, and attention.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
