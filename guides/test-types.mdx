## Key differences between tracks

| Aspect | Preescolar | Escolar |
|--------|------------|---------|
| Age range | 3–6 years | 7–12 years |
| Total max score | 252 | 252 |
| Alimentación factors | 3 | 4 (adds Propiocepción) |
| Higiene factors | 3 | 4 (adds Ducha/baño, splits continence) |
| Vestido factors | 1 (General) | 2 (Tareas aisladas + Vestido completo) |
| Funciones Ejecutivas Básicas max | 24 | 30 |
| Funciones Ejecutivas de Orden Superior max | 27 | 24 |

<Info>
  Both tracks share the same four-phase structure and the same risk classification thresholds. The differences reflect the developmental expectations appropriate for each age group.
</Info>

<Note>
  The total maximum score is 252 for both tracks, which means results are directly comparable in percentage terms across age groups.
</Note>
